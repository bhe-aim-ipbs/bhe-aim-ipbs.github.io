Zeyu Chen received the M.Eng. degree from the College of Automation Engineering, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), Jiangsu, China, in 2024. He is currently working towards the D.S. degree at the Technische Universität München (TUM), Germany. His research interests include signal processing and deep learning for the application in diagnosis of mental disorders.
