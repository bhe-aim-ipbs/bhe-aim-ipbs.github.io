Yifan Feng received the B. S. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology (School of Cyberspace Security), Xinjiang University, Urumqi, China, in 2024. He is currently working towards the master degree at the School of Medical Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China. In 2026, he will start his integrated master's and doctoral program at the Beijing Institute of Technology (Zhuhai), Zhuhai, China. His research interests include Federated Learning Paradigms for Medical Applications and Affective Computing, Information Fusion, Continual Learning.
