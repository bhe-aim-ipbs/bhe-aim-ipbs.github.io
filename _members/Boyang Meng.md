Boyang Meng is an undergraduate student (Class of 2023) at Beijing Institute of Technology, majoring in Computer Science and Technology. His research focuses on scarce body-sound recognition, with particular interest in data-limited learning scenarios for physiological and biomedical signals. His academic interests include audio signal processing, deep learning for small-sample classification, and machine learning methods for healthcare applications.
